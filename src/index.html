About Us


ARIES mining was founded on 21st July 2100 in Columbia Hills, Gusev - Mars and is now one of the largest mining companies. Our purpose is to build a better world by bringing people and resources a bit closer. We try this through our strategy to deliver long-term value and returns through the cycle. We produce essential commodities through our assets, including iron ore, copper, nickel, potash, bauxite, phosphorous rock, gypsum. We are proud of everything we have achieved. At ARIES, we know our future is even brighter than our past.

Our Goals


ARIES is committed to advancing everyday life by supplying the commodities needed to develop, sustain and improve the world around us. Our state-of-the-art machinery makes it possible to reach the farthest asteroids and planets in our solar system.
Through the scale and diversity of our mining methods, we responsibly supply the commodities that are fundamental to the building blocks of our inter-planetary system. Be that to build essential infrastructure, from homes and hospitals, to roads and rail networks; or to ensure access to affordable energy for all.
